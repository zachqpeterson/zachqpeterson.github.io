Daycare Descent

GitHub: ProjectPascal

Daycare Descent is a team-based FPS about surviving against a horde of toys. I've worked on a lot of the systems in the game, including networking, level mechanics, UI design, particles, and other gameplay mechanics. This game is released on steam for free, try it out to see what five students can do in 10 weeks.
